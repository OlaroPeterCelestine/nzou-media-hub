Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some common questions we receive. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us directly.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for most projects. For larger events or complex productions, we suggest 6-8 weeks notice to ensure availability and proper planning.

Do you provide equipment?

Yes, we provide all necessary professional equipment including cameras, lighting, audio gear, and post-production tools. Our equipment is regularly maintained and updated to ensure the highest quality results.

What's included in your packages?

Our packages include shooting time, basic editing, high-resolution digital files, and a consultation. Additional services like advanced editing, prints, or rush delivery can be added for an extra fee.

Do you travel for projects?

Yes, we travel throughout Zimbabwe and can accommodate international projects. Travel costs are calculated based on distance and project requirements.

How long does post-production take?

Post-production timelines vary by project complexity. Simple photo editing typically takes 3-5 business days, while video projects may take 1-3 weeks depending on the scope and requirements.
